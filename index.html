About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at IIT Indore. My research interests include algorithms, algebraic graph theory, linear algebra, and combinatorial optimization.

I have also worked as a Visiting Scholar at Purdue University, USA, where I worked under Prof. Vaneet Aggarwal on stronger approximation guarantees for non-monotone γ-weakly DR-submodular maximization.
